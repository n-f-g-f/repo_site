Game Plan in 4 Keys

  • Set the tempo early: if you let them run into flow, the game can speed up on their terms.
  • Finish possessions: the first stop is not enough against this team because the offensive glass is a real source of extra points.
  • Load into A. Broussard: he is the main organizer, so make secondary creators finish possessions.
  • Closeout discipline matters: D. Gameiro, J. Silva are the first perimeter names you cannot lose in help situations.

Scouting Notes

Offense

  • The main offensive references are A. Broussard and G. Crandall; load the scouting plan into those names first.

Perimeter Shooting

  • Priority closeouts start with D. Gameiro, J. Silva, and M. Romdhane; do not lose them in help-and-recover situations.
  • Some perimeter volume comes from G. Crandall and K. McEwen; contest, but do not over-help into secondary advantages.

Scoring Areas

  • Their offense naturally flows toward Restricted Area; protect that area early in possessions.
  • Several players have clear comfort zones, especially R. Yussuf, A. Dziewa, and A. Broussard; force them away from preferred areas.

Units
