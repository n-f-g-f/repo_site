Model notes

Offense = baseline ORtg + shot mix effect + four factors offensive effects + lineup offense effect.

Defense = baseline DRtg + defensive factor effects + lineup defense effect.

Lineup effects use shrunk lineup ratings: shrunk = (m / (m + 60)) × lineup rating + (60 / (m + 60)) × team baseline.

This keeps tiny-sample lineups from pretending to be miracles.

What changed in v2

• DRtg is no longer fixed.

• Top lineups can be reweighted interactively.

• The page now shows offensive, defensive and lineup contributions separately.
