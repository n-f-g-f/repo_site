Model notes

ORtg simulation formula

1) Rebuild shot mix and compute expected shot points per shot from team zone PPS.

2) Apply deltas to baseline four factors.

3) Estimate offensive lift as the sum of:

• shot mix effect = (sim shot PPS − base shot PPS) × FGA per 100 possessions

• eFG effect = ΔeFG × 100 × 1.20

• TOV effect = −ΔTOV × 100 × 0.80

• ORB effect = ΔORB × 100 × 0.55

• FTR effect = ΔFTR × 100 × 0.35

v1 keeps DRtg fixed and turns the page into a clean offensive simulation sandbox first.
