How They Play

Tempo
League-average pace; rhythm control matters more than pure speed against them.
Ball Movement
Above-average assist profile; expect cleaner passing sequences and more extra-pass possessions.
Offensive Glass
Second-chance threat; the first stop is not enough against this team.
Turnovers
Better-than-average ball security; they do not hand away many live possessions.
Physicality
Foul profile sits near league average; physicality is part of the game, but not their defining trait.
Shot Mix
Above-average three-point volume; spacing discipline and clean closeouts matter.

Game Plan in 4 Keys

  • Own the rhythm: pace itself is not the main separator, so the quality of possessions matters more than speed.
  • Finish possessions: the first stop is not enough against this team because the offensive glass is a real source of extra points.
  • Stay connected to spacing: their perimeter volume can bend the floor if your help and recovery are sloppy.
  • Know the first reference: T. Carter is a paint finisher, and the plan should start with taking away his comfort.

Scouting Notes

Scouting Notes

Offense

  • The main offensive references are T. Carter and M. Romdhane; load the scouting plan into those names first.

Perimeter Shooting

  • Priority closeouts start with J. Silva, D. Gameiro, and B. Gomes; do not lose them in help-and-recover situations.
  • Some perimeter volume comes from A. Broussard and T. Drechsel III; contest, but do not over-help into secondary advantages.

Scoring Areas

  • Their offense naturally flows toward Above the Break 3; protect that area early in possessions.
  • Several players have clear comfort zones, especially T. Carter, T. Douglas, and J. Silva; force them away from preferred areas.

Units

  • Most-used lineup to rehearse first: A. Broussard | J. Silva | M. Romdhane | T. Carter | T. Douglas.
  • Secondary lineup to recognize: A. Broussard | B. Gomes | J. Silva | T. Carter | T. Douglas.
  • Recognize the main duo/trio combinations: A. Broussard | T. Carter and A. Broussard | T. Carter | T. Douglas.

Defensive Emphasis

  • Second-chance threat; the first stop is not enough against this team.
  • Do not lose J. Silva and D. Gameiro in help-and-recover situations.
