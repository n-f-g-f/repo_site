Scouting Notes

Scouting Notes

Offense

  • The main offensive references are T. Filipe and J. Mendes; load the scouting plan into those names first.

Perimeter Shooting

  • Priority closeouts start with A. Martins, S. Sousa, and J. Mendes; do not lose them in help-and-recover situations.
  • Some perimeter volume comes from T. Filipe and F. Simoes; contest, but do not over-help into secondary advantages.

Scoring Areas

  • Their offense naturally flows toward Paint Non-RA; protect that area early in possessions.
  • Several players have clear comfort zones, especially J. Andrade, F. Simoes, and T. Filipe; force them away from preferred areas.

Units

  • Most-used lineup to rehearse first: A. Martins | J. Andrade | J. Mendes | P. Pardal Santos | T. Filipe.
  • Secondary lineup to recognize: A. Martins | J. Andrade | J. Mendes | T. Filipe | T. Silva.
  • Recognize the main duo/trio combinations: J. Andrade | T. Filipe and A. Martins | J. Mendes | T. Filipe.

Defensive Emphasis

  • Offensive rebounding is around league average; box-outs matter but this is not their main edge.
  • Do not lose A. Martins and S. Sousa in help-and-recover situations.
