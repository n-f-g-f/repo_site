Game Plan in 4 Keys

  • Set the tempo early: if you let them run into flow, the game can speed up on their terms.
  • Finish possessions: the first stop is not enough against this team because the offensive glass is a real source of extra points.
  • Know the first reference: S. Suggs is a movement shooter, and the plan should start with taking away his comfort.
  • Closeout discipline matters: S. Suggs, J. Costa are the first perimeter names you cannot lose in help situations.

  • The main offensive references are S. Suggs and M. Lamut; load the scouting plan into those names first.

Perimeter Shooting

  • Priority closeouts start with S. Suggs, J. Costa, and J. Rodrigues; do not lose them in help-and-recover situations.
  • Some perimeter volume comes from M. Rodrigues and J. Rodrigues; contest, but do not over-help into secondary advantages.

Scoring Areas

  • Their offense naturally flows toward Paint Non-RA; protect that area early in possessions.
  • Several players have clear comfort zones, especially S. Suggs, M. Lamut, and J. Okemirie; force them away from preferred areas.
