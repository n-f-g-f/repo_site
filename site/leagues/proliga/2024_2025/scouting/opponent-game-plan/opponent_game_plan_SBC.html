How They Play

Tempo
Below-average pace; they are more comfortable when the game stays organized in the half court.
Ball Movement
Below-average assist profile; more possessions end through direct creation than collective flow.
Offensive Glass
Offensive rebounding is around league average; box-outs matter but this is not their main edge.
Turnovers
Better-than-average ball security; they do not hand away many live possessions.
Physicality
Lower foul volume than league average; this is not a team that wants constant contact chaos.
Shot Mix
Less three-point dependent than league average; more of their offense is built away from pure perimeter volume.

Game Plan in 4 Keys

  • Control the rhythm: they are more comfortable in a controlled half-court game, so do not let them play cleanly into structure.
  • Do not overreact to the arc: make them finish over traffic instead of giving up easy paint comfort.
  • Know the first reference: A. Brito is a inside scorer, and the plan should start with taking away his comfort.
  • Closeout discipline matters: D. Mitchell, G. Saiote are the first perimeter names you cannot lose in help situations.

Scouting Notes

Scouting Notes

Offense

  • The main offensive references are A. Brito and D. Runge; load the scouting plan into those names first.

Perimeter Shooting

  • Priority closeouts start with D. Mitchell, G. Saiote, and B. Almeida; do not lose them in help-and-recover situations.
  • Some perimeter volume comes from D. Goncalves and E. Tome; contest, but do not over-help into secondary advantages.

Scoring Areas

  • Their offense naturally flows toward Paint Non-RA; protect that area early in possessions.
  • Several players have clear comfort zones, especially A. Brito, G. Saiote, and D. Goncalves; force them away from preferred areas.

Units

  • Most-used lineup to rehearse first: A. Brito | D. Goncalves | D. Runge | E. Tome | G. Saiote.
  • Secondary lineup to recognize: A. Brito | D. Goncalves | D. Runge | E. Tome | J. Lewis.
  • Recognize the main duo/trio combinations: A. Brito | D. Runge and A. Brito | D. Runge | E. Tome.

Defensive Emphasis

  • Offensive rebounding is around league average; box-outs matter but this is not their main edge.
  • Do not lose D. Mitchell and G. Saiote in help-and-recover situations.
