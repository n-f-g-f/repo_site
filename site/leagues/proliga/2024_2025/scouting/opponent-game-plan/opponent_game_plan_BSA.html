How They Play

Tempo
Below-average pace; they are more comfortable when the game stays organized in the half court.
Ball Movement
Ball movement is close to league average; they can share it but are not purely pass-first.
Offensive Glass
Limited offensive rebounding impact; finishing the first contest usually kills the possession.
Turnovers
Loose enough with the ball to be pressured; there are possessions to steal here.
Physicality
Foul profile sits near league average; physicality is part of the game, but not their defining trait.
Shot Mix
Above-average three-point volume; spacing discipline and clean closeouts matter.

Game Plan in 4 Keys

  • Control the rhythm: they are more comfortable in a controlled half-court game, so do not let them play cleanly into structure.
  • Win the first action: they do not consistently hurt teams with second-chance volume, so clean first stops carry value.
  • Stay connected to spacing: their perimeter volume can bend the floor if your help and recovery are sloppy.
  • Know the first reference: B. Free is a movement shooter, and the plan should start with taking away his comfort.

Scouting Notes

Scouting Notes

Offense

  • The main offensive references are A. Lopes and B. Free; load the scouting plan into those names first.

Perimeter Shooting

  • Priority closeouts start with B. Free, A. Lopes, and J. Oliveira; do not lose them in help-and-recover situations.
  • Some perimeter volume comes from H. Pereira and D. Horta; contest, but do not over-help into secondary advantages.

Scoring Areas

  • Their offense naturally flows toward Above the Break 3; protect that area early in possessions.
  • Several players have clear comfort zones, especially B. Free, H. Pereira, and A. Lopes; force them away from preferred areas.

Units

  • Most-used lineup to rehearse first: A. Lopes | B. Free | D. Horta | H. Pereira | S. Quissanga.
  • Secondary lineup to recognize: A. Lopes | B. Free | D. Horta | H. Pereira | S. Araujo.
  • Recognize the main duo/trio combinations: A. Lopes | B. Free and A. Lopes | B. Free | H. Pereira.

Defensive Emphasis

  • Limited offensive rebounding impact; finishing the first contest usually kills the possession.
  • Do not lose B. Free and A. Lopes in help-and-recover situations.
