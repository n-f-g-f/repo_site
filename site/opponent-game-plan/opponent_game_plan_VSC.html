Scouting Notes

Offense

  • C. Jenkins is their primary scoring option (18.2 ppg).

Perimeter Shooting

  • Priority closeouts vs. C. Jenkins, P. Pinto, F. Gewert, S. Verde because of their 3-point volume and efficiency.
  • J. Baker, S. Peek, P. Bastos, J. Sangha generate high 3-point volume, but the efficiency is manageable.

Finishing Areas

  • Their highest-volume team zone is Restricted Area.
  • Players such as S. Peek, M. Jacobs, J. Sangha, C. Jenkins, J. Baker, F. Gewert, P. Pinto, A. Mendes look to finish in their preferred areas.

Units

  • Base lineup: B. Araujo | C. Jenkins | J. Sangha | M. Jacobs | S. Peek — tends to stabilize both offense and defense.
  • Alternate lineup: B. Araujo | C. Jenkins | J. Sangha | S. Peek | S. Verde gives them a different look.

Defensive Emphasis

  • Controlling the perimeter shooting of C. Jenkins, P. Pinto, F. Gewert, S. Verde is a primary focus.
  • Protect Restricted Area because of their collective shot volume there.
