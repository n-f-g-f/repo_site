How the platform works

The public homepage should explain the structure clearly while keeping protected areas clearly gated.

Step 1

Login

Access starts with account login. Once authenticated, the platform applies the leagues included in the active account.

Step 2

Choose a league

Start with the competition you want to work on. Each league page becomes the entry point for its available seasons.

Step 3

Choose a season

Within each league, open the season you need and then move into Analytics, General or Scouting for that exact context.

Access model

Four access plans

One League, Two Leagues, All Leagues and Full Access Yearly. Access is enforced after login and applied according to the active entitlement.
